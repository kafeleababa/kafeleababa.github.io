Margins, Padding and Borders

Margins

Margins are used as to define the space between elements on your page

Padding

If Margins are used to define the spacing of the areas outside of the elements, padding is used to define the spacing on the inside of the element. Used in conjunction with each other, margins and padding are really useful when designing the layout of your pages.

Borders

Borders are used to visually show the edges of an area or element.
